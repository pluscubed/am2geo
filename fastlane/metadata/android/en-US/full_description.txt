When you open an Apple Maps link, redirect it to your default maps app (such as Google Maps)

Features:
- Supports all Apple Maps links, including short-links
- Preserve places, coordinates, searches, and directions
- Keep using your preferred maps app on Android
- No ads, accounts, analytics, or tracking SDKs
